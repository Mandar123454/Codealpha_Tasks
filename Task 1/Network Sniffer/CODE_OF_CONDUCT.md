## Enforcement

### Reporting a Violation

If you witness or experience a violation:

1. **Document** the incident (screenshots, dates, context)
2. **Report** to project maintainers at [maintainer-email@example.com]
3. **Include** relevant details and context
4. **Avoid** public confrontation when possible

**All reports will be:**
- Treated with confidentiality
- Investigated thoroughly
- Handled promptly and fairly

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** - First-time minor violations
2. **Temporary Suspension** - Repeated or serious violations
3. **Permanent Ban** - Severe or repeated harassment
4. **Legal Action** - If applicable and necessary
